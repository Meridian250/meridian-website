Who we are

A trusted bridge between UK suppliers
and GCC buyers

Meridian Distributors is a UK-based international trading company specialising in the sourcing and distribution of premium health supplement brands to the UAE and broader GCC region.

We work directly with authorised UK wholesalers to supply retailers, gyms, pharmacies, and online stores with brands their customers already know and trust — at competitive trade pricing with reliable, consistent supply.

Our model is straightforward: we bridge the gap between abundant, competitively-priced UK supply and high-demand GCC markets, offering buyers easy access to European health brands without the complexity of international procurement.

Authentic UK sourcing

All products sourced directly from authorised UK wholesalers and brand distributors. Full traceability and documentation available on request.

Flexible order quantities

We work with businesses at every stage — from independent gyms placing their first trial order to established distributors requiring container volumes.

Fast, reliable logistics

Air freight for speed-sensitive orders, sea freight for volume. Fully tracked shipments with dedicated support from order to delivery.
